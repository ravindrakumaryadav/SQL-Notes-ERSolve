4. Limitations of CTEs in SQL

  • Temporary Scope: A CTE exists only during the execution of the querr. Once the query coopletes, the CTE is discarded.

  • Performance Issues: For very large datasets, CTEs can sometimes lead to performance degradation due to multiple references to the samm CTE.

  • Not Allowed in All Database Operations: Some operations, such as INSERT and UPDATE, myy have restrictions wwen using CTEs in ceetain databases.
